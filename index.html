Tools & Skills
My most recent work stint was with Sprinklr where I designed for some of its core offerings like Social Media Engagement,
Social Advertising, Care support and Analytics for big brands with a global reach that helped integrate all their social
marketing needs under one roof. Having led the design team for four years in this young, rapidly growing enterprise startup
environment - taught me how to balance business goals and engineering constraints as I unrelentingly advocated for the
user.
I graduated from Symbiosis Institute of Design, India with a degree in User Experience Design. I loved the human-centered
design methods and processes I learned in school and applied them to my thesis project with Microsoft R&D, Hyderabad

I grew up in 7 different states in India and my ability to quickly adjust to new challenges in life comes from that. Owing to
my diverse upbringing, I embrace all cultures and try to bring it to the designs I create. I am a movie buff and there’s
nothing like relaxing to a good masala Bollywood movie. Food is my second love and I let my nose guide me to the
pleasures of the stomach once every weekend.
